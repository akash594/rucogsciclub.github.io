RUTGERS Cognitive Science Club

About

The Rutgers Cognitive Science club is home to a variety of fields: computer science, linguistics, psychology, philosophy, neuroscience and more! Every member contributes their own unique expertise to our weekly meetings, where we talk about Cognitive Science topics, have debates about artificial intelligence and learn about research opportunities at Rutgers. Other events we've hosted include career panels, guest lectures from professors and programming bootcamps. We're also big fans of the TV show Black Mirror!

Our meetings for the Fall 2018 semester are every Wednesday from 8:30 to 10:00pm in the Academic Building Learning Center on College Avenue. New members are always welcome. To learn more, email us at rutgerscognitivescienceclub@gmail.com or contact the club president.
