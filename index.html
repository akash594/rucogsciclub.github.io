About

Who We Are:

The Rutgers Cognitive Science Club is a student run organization at Rutgers University. The members of our club are people passionate about the field of Cognitive Science, and interested in impacting the world through a deeper understanding of the brain and mind. To that end, our focus is to provide a rich, diverse community to encourage the development of the personal, academic and professional life of undergraduate students in Cognitive Science. For more information about the cognitive science program here at Rutgers, check out our department's website:
https://ruccs.rutgers.edu

What We Do:

Our club runs multiple events throughout the semester. We bring in professors, graduate students, and industry professionals to talk about their research and the work they do in the field. We also host panels to debate cutting edge topics. We run a weekly bootcamp to teach people Python programming and Data Science. If you have any ideas for events you would like to see happen, feel free to reach out to us (contact information below)!
