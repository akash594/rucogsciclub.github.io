About Us

Who We Are:

The Rutgers Cognitive Science Club is a student run organization at Rutgers University. The members of our club are people passionate about the field of Cognitive Science, and intersted in impacting the world through a deeper underestanding of the brain and mind.

To that end, our focus is to provide a rich, diverse community to encourage the development of the personal, academic, and professional life of undergraduate students in Cognitive Sciences. What We Do:

Our club runs multiple events throughout the semester. We bring in professors, graduate students, and industry professionals to talk about their research and the work they do in the field. We also host panels to debate cutting edge topics, mentorship programs for everyone from incoming freshman to rising seniors. We also emphasize building real-world projects! We provide access to top-of-the-line VR equipment, an Emotiv 14 channel EEG headset, 3D printing, and more!
