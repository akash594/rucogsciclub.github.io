Mentorship

Mentorship is a cornerstone of our club's mission. We believe that the more we give back to the members of our community, the more we get. Our mentorship program is designed to help you meet fellow students in the major or related disciplines, and give you insight into things like how to find research opportunities, scheduling classes, and how to make the most out of your time at Rutgers. Most of all, you get the opportunity to meet some incredibly cool students and make lasting friendships!

Click here to sign up for our mentorship program!

Tech

As a club we're a big believer in learning by doing. Especially in technical fields, a number of students have a difficult time figuring out where to start when they're looking to add some projects to their resume. We have a large number of tech projects in the works this semester! Some of the projects include:
  • Improving the website!
  • Renovating the Hackerspace
  • Creating our very own Artechouse
  • A data science bootcamp!

Conference

We are in the process of planning the first ever undergraduate Cognitive Science conference at Rutgers. In partnership with the Center for Cognitive Science and our very own international undergraduate journal in Cognitive Science: The Mental Note, we want to bring some of the best names in cognitive science together at Rutgers to help students network, present their research, and promote their brand.

Click here if you're interested in joining the conference planning committee!
