Events

We hold general events every Wednesday from 8:30 - 9:30 PM. Unless otherwise specified, our meeting location is:
  • Room 1125
  • Academic Building (West Wing)
  • College Avenue Campus
Our events range from community building activities (like our Mentorship and Mocktails event!) to speaker series events or career panels. This coming semester we have speakers flying in from London and Seattle from companies like Facebook, Spotify, researchers, neuroscientists, neurosurgeons, perceptual scientists, people who work in neuroprosthetics, neurologists, UI/UX designers and more!
